How to find the voicebank you need

This webpage is designed to easily download any Thea voicebank. To find a specific voicebank just right for your needs, you can describe features like language (German, English...), voicebank type (CV, CVVC...) or append name (Contralto, Crystal...) to the "Quick Search"-tool. If you need further information on the voicebanks, just go through the descriptions attatched below.

If you are concerned some of the work you plan to do with her might be not appropiate to me, feel free to look up the Terms of Use or simply contact me.

Have fun and don't forget to send me your amazing work!
